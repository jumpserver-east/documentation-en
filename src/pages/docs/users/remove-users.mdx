# Remove users from organization <Icon name="xpack"/>

## Remove a user

<Steps>

<div>
<Nav name="console" />
</div>

<div>
In the left menu, click **<Icon name="UserO" /> Users**.
</div>

<div>
In the table, find the user you want to remove.
</div>

<div>
In the **Actions** column, click the <Icon name="..." /> icon, then click **Remove**.
</div>

<div>
In the pop-up window, confirm the information to be removed, and then click **OK**.
</div>

</Steps>


## Bulk remove users

<Steps>

<div>
<Nav name="console" />
</div>

<div>
In the left menu, click **<Icon name="UserO" /> Users**.
</div>

<div>
In the table, check the users you want to remove.
</div>

<div>
Above the table, select **Actions <Icon name="chevron-down" />** button, then click **<Icon name="CircleMinus" /> Remove selected**.
</div>

</Steps>
