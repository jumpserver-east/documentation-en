# Delete users

## Delete a user

<Steps>

<div>
<Nav name="console" />
</div>

<div>
In the left menu, click **<Icon name="UserO" /> Users**.
</div>

<div>
In the table, find the user you want to delete.
</div>

<div>
In the **Actions** column, click the <Icon name="..." /> icon, then click **Delete**.
</div>

<div>
In the pop-up window, confirm the information to be deleted, and then click **OK**.
</div>

</Steps>


## Bulk delete users

<Steps>

<div>
<Nav name="console" />
</div>

<div>
In the left menu, click **<Icon name="UserO" /> Users**.
</div>

<div>
In the table, check the users you want to delete.
</div>

<div>
Above the table, select **Actions <Icon name="chevron-down" />** button, then click **<Icon name="Trash2" /> Delete selected**.
</div>

<div>
In the pop-up window, confirm the information to be deleted, and then click **OK**.
</div>

</Steps>
